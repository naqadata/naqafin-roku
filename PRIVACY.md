# Naqafin Privacy

Naqafin is an independent, unofficial Roku client for connecting to a Jellyfin-compatible media server that you configure.

Naqafin does not sell personal information, display ads, or include third-party analytics, telemetry, or crash-reporting services controlled by Naqadata.

The app stores and uses connection details, authentication tokens, playback state, settings, and media information needed to communicate with your configured server. Saved login tokens and settings are stored on the Roku device. Your Jellyfin password is used for sign-in but is not stored by Naqafin.

Your server controls the media library, user accounts, metadata, playback history, and related data available through the app.

Roku may collect device, platform, and usage information under Roku's own privacy policies.

Naqafin is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or officially supported by Jellyfin or Roku.
